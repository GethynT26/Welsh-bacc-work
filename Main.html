Signs of declining health and wellbeing

Signs that your's or others health and wellbeing are declining can vary but often there are both physical and emotional symptons. Physically, people may struggle to sleep, frequent headaches and fatigue. While emootionally, they may feel anxious, sad, irritable and often will lose interests in hobbies they once enjoyed. Some people may engage in unhealthy coping behaviors, like smoking, taking substances and etc. Its important to recongise these signs early and try to find support to help improve your well-being

You should also encourage them to do healthy habits, like regular physical acitivites, which then you could join them in, whether its cycling, a team sport, jogging, etc. This creates a stronger bond between you and the other and allows them to look up to something.
