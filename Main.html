How can you improve your health and wellbeing?

There are many ways of improving your health and wellbeing, like: Exercising, The recommened time of exercise, each week is atleast 150 minutes for a adult but for a young pupil, you should aim for 60 minutes of exercise a day. Doing exercise will help release stress, build muscle and influence growth in the body and mind. Quality sleep, Quality sleep is important as it allows your body and mind to recharge for the next day, you should aim for atleast 7 hours and more. Hobbies, Hobbies can help improve your health and wellbeing as hobbies are something you enjoy and can look forward to in the day to keep you going throughout. Hobbies can be individual or not, Whatever you enjoy is a hobby.
