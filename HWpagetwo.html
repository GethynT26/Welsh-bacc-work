Young minds

Young minds are a Uk mental health charity, that dedicate in supporting children and young people with mental health challenges. The provide guides and support for young pupils, parents and teachers, while covering topics on anxiety, depression, bullying and etc. Youngminds encourages youth to understand their emotions and to access help when needed, to create positive changes in their mental health.
